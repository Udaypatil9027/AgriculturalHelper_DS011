Connecting Farmers with Agricultural Experts

AgriHelp is a platform where farmers can share crop disease problems and get expert solutions from qualified agronomists. Together, we're building a stronger agricultural community.

How AgriHelp Works

Farmers Report Issues

Farmers sign up and describe their crop problems, upload photos, and provide details about their farming conditions.

Agronomists Analyze

Qualified agronomists review the problems, diagnose issues, and provide expert recommendations.

Solutions Provided

Farmers receive actionable advice to address their agricultural challenges and improve crop health.
